Week 1 · Foundation — make it safe + persistent
Milestone 11

INF-1 · Workspace sync

Foundation

The single biggest launch blocker. Arcs, notebook entries, and sub-theory drafts are localStorage-only — only books touch Firestore. A second user who switches devices or clears cache loses everything they wrote. This puts the private workspace behind per-user Firestore sync.

Sub-stages

Milestone 12

INF-2 · Multi-user isolation

Foundation

Make the app genuinely single-tenant-per-user. resolveActiveUid currently warns "multiple users not supported" and falls back to the first user alphabetically. Every read and write must be scoped to the signed-in uid before a second person logs in.

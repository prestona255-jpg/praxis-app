A Pedagogy of Desire · sub-theory
You can't desire what you can't be recognized in
Public registerIntellectual register

Desire is not only wanting — it is wanting toward a world that can hold you. To be recognized is the first light; to recognize another is the second.

Where recognition is withheld, desire bends around the absence, learning to want what will be permitted rather than what is true.
